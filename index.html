About Me

Greetings! My name is Chris Ramer. I do full stack programming and am an indie game developer. I have been coding since 2017 and have since learned several skills for both front and back ends.


On the side, I'm a gamer and modder. I try to use my abstract sense of humor to make everyone's lives a little better! :)


I may come off as immature but honestly, that's because I'm human. I don't want to be some robot with no emotion. Human connection is a big part of what makes life worth living. If you want to hire a robot, you'll have to go to someone else.
